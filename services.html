About WiiLink24

WiiLink24 is a revival service for the now-defunct Japanese exclusive Wii channels. This service will (soon) allow you to use Wii no Ma, Demae Channel, and Digicam Print Channel.

2020-2021 WiiLink24 Team

We are not associated with Nintendo nor its affiliates. We are also not associated with the staff of any other online services for the Wii.
